# FREE INTEGRATIONS FOR BROFIT

## ANALYTICS & MONITORING
1. **Google Analytics 4 (GA4)**
   - **Cost**: Free
   - **Use**: Track user visits, behavior, and demographics.
   - **Integration**: `@next/third-parties` package.

2. **Vercel Analytics**
   - **Cost**: Free (Hobby Tier)
   - **Use**: Privacy-friendly visitor tracking.
   - **Integration**: Enable in Vercel dashboard.

3. **Sentry**
   - **Cost**: Free Tier (dev plan)
   - **Use**: Real-time error tracking and performance monitoring.
   - **Integration**: `@sentry/nextjs`.

4. **Microsoft Clarity**
   - **Cost**: Generally Free
   - **Use**: Heatmaps and session recordings to see how users click/scroll.
   - **Integration**: Simple script tag.

## COMMUNICATION
5. **Tawk.to**
   - **Cost**: Free
   - **Use**: Live chat widget to talk to visitors in real-time.
   - **Integration**: Script tag.

6. **Formspree**
   - **Cost**: Free Tier (50 submissions/month)
   - **Use**: Handle contact form submissions without a backend.
   - **Integration**: Simple form action URL.

## CONTENT & MARKETING
7. **Mailchimp**
   - **Cost**: Free Tier (1000 sends/month)
   - **Use**: Newsletter collection and email marketing.
   - **Integration**: API or embedded form.

8. **Google Maps Embed**
   - **Cost**: Free (Unlimited for basic embed)
   - **Use**: Show gym location on Contact page.
   - **Integration**: Embedding iframe.

## UTILITIES
9. **Cloudinary**
   - **Cost**: Free Tier
   - **Use**: Hosting and optimizing specialized images/videos if Vercel bandwidth gets low.
   - **Integration**: API.
